Dataset Used in the Project

The dataset used in this project was obtained from the Kaggle platform and contains information about traffic accidents recorded by DETRAN in Brazil during 2020. It was shared by Raphael Marconato, aiming to promote studies focused on analyzing and predicting accident severity.

The dataset contains 22,644 records distributed across 18 columns, of which 14 are categorical variables. The data is organized to allow statistical analysis and predictive model building.

Main Variables in the Dataset

  • num_boletim: Police report number.
  • data_hora_boletim: Date and time of the report.
  • nÂº_envolvido: Number of people involved.
  • condutor: Indicates if the driver was responsible for the accident.
  • desc_severidade: Indicates whether the accident was fatal or not.
  • sexo: Gender of the person involved.
  • cinto_seguranca: Seat belt usage.
  • embriaguez: Driver's alcohol condition.
  • idade / nascimento: Age group and birth date.
  • categoria_habilitacao / descricao_habilitacao: License category and description.
  • declaracao_obito: Death declaration in the accident.
  • cod_severidade_antiga: Previous severity code.
  • especie_veiculo: Type of vehicle involved.
  • pedestre / passageiro: Involvement of pedestrians and passengers.

Why Was This Dataset Chosen?

This dataset stands out for its level of detail and organization. It enables deep descriptive analyses and the development of predictive models focused on accident severity. Its granularity is ideal for understanding the factors that influence traffic accident outcomes in Brazil.

Source and Dataset Information

  • Name: DETRAN Accidents - 2020
  • Author: Raphael Marconato
  • Platform: Kaggle
  • Records: 22,644
  • Columns: 18 (14 categorical)
